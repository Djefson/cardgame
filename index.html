Cards Guessing Game

Introduction

Welcome to the Card Guessing Game! This game tests your ability to predict whether the next card will be higher or lower than the current card. Your goal is to guess correctly until there are no more cards left in the deck.

Instructions

  1. When the game starts, a deck of cards is shuffled, and the first card is drawn.
  2. The current card is displayed on the screen.
  3. You have three options to make your guess:
    • Guess Higher: Click the "Higher" button if you think the next card will have a higher value than the current card.
    • Guess Lower: Click the "Lower" button if you think the next card will have a lower value than the current card.
    • Guess Same: Click the "Same" button if you think the next card will have the same value as the current card.
  4. After making your guess, the next card is drawn from the deck and revealed.
  5. If your guess is correct:
    • If there are no more cards left in the deck, you win the game!
    • Otherwise, you can continue making guesses for the next card.
  6. If your guess is incorrect, the game is over, and you lose.
  7. You can restart the game at any time by clicking the "Restart" button.

Game Controls

  • "HIGH" button: Click this button to guess that the next card will have a higher value.
  • "LOW" button: Click this button to guess that the next card will have a lower value.
  • "SAME" button: Click this button to guess that the next card will have the same value.
  • "Restart" button: Click this button to restart the game after winning or guessing wrong.

Game Progress

  • The game is in progress until you win or guess wrong.
  • While the game is in progress, the game buttons are enabled.
  • After winning or guessing wrong, the game buttons are disabled until you restart the game.

Winning the Game

To win the game, you need to guess correctly until there are no more cards left in the deck. If you achieve this, a congratulatory message will be displayed, and the result will start blinking. Additionally, a sound effect will play to celebrate your victory.

Guessing Wrong

If you guess wrong at any point during the game, a message will be displayed, indicating that the game is over. You will have the option to restart the game and try again.

Have Fun!

Enjoy playing the Card Guessing Game and challenge yourself to make accurate predictions. Good luck and have fun!
